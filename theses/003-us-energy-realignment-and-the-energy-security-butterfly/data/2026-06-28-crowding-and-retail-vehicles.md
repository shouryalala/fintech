- 🔴 Market is structurally **OVERSUPPLIED** — IEA: ~114 mbd capacity by 2030, **~8 mbd above demand**; demand peaking ~2030. Any chokepoint premium fights a deflationary tide.

- 🔴 Precedent: the 1980s Tanker War produced a transient premium while real oil prices **fell through the decade.**
- 🟡 Steelman: IEA's forecast surpluses have repeatedly under-delivered (OilPrice, NY Fed) — so hold the mean-reversion case as *likely*, not certain.
