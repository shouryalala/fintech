## Read
- **SCHD = best single shield.** Diversification + lowest single-name risk + income cushion =
  most robust when the *path* of the bust is unknown. Confirmed clean of AI-infra.
- **XLV = best pure-defense complement** (only zero-AI, recession-resilient sector), but a
  concentrated LLY bet at a full multiple.
- **XLF = weakest AI-recession shield** despite cheapest — dodges the AI *narrative*, not the
  *recession* leg. Deep value for "economy holds," not for "survive the bust."
- **Robust combo:** SCHD core + XLV defensive tilt; XLF as post-downturn value, not pre-bust hedge.
- **Caveat (unchanged):** none is a true hedge — in a liquidation correlations →1 and all fall,
  just less, with faster recovery + income. Actual crash-positive = Treasuries/cash/puts, not these.
